INSTRUCTIONS

Ping pong plays with the following instruction:

    If you enter a number in the input area and submit,it will count up the numbers from 1 upto your number but with the following exceptions:
  • Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become "pingpong"
